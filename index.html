Our Mission

eXipini is dedicated to bridging the gap between talented rural weavers and customers who appreciate authentic, handcrafted textiles. We specialize in gamusa - traditional Assamese textiles that represent centuries of cultural heritage and artistic excellence.

Our platform empowers rural artisans by providing them with a direct connection to markets, ensuring fair compensation for their exceptional craftsmanship while preserving traditional weaving techniques for future generations.
